世界初のサスペンション技術開発や熱管理システム設計など、機械×制御×データを横断した開発経験を持つ。

[At the Australian Embassy] 
Presenting Our Vision for the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge

We were recently honored to be invited to an event at the Australian Embassy as the Kogakuin University Solar Team.
We had the privilege of presenting our activity report and our vision for the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge 2025 to His Excellency Mr. Justin Hayhurst, Australian Ambassador to Japan, Mr. Shuichi Ishibashi, Global CEO of Bridgestone Corporation, and other distinguished guests.

As I mentioned in my speech, this grueling 3,000km solar car race is never just about crossing a finish line.

"The sun gave us energy. But it was people who gave us the courage to keep going."

Beyond the technical engineering challenges, I have reaffirmed that the true value of this project lies in learning, personal growth, and the "human connections" that transcend borders.

While my journey with the team concludes with this tournament, the Kogakuin University Solar Team is already looking ahead to the 2027 challenge, preparing to reach even greater heights.
